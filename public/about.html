About The North Face

Equipping adventurers since 1966

Our Story

The North Face was founded in 1966 in San Francisco as a small mountaineering retailer. What started as a passion for the outdoors has grown into a global leader in outdoor apparel, footwear, and equipment.

Our mission has always been simple: to equip adventurers with the gear they need to explore the world safely and comfortably. From climbers ascending the world's highest peaks to hikers exploring local trails, our products are designed for those who refuse to be confined by walls.
